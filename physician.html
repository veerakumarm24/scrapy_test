Dr. Ahila Subramanian, MD is a Allergy & Immunology Specialist in Strongsville, OH and has over 13 years of experience in the medical field. Dr. Subramanian has more experience with Environmental Allergies, Food and Drug Allergies, and Upper Respiratory Conditions than other specialists in her area. She graduated from University of Cincinnati / Main Campus medical school in 2007. She is affiliated with Cleveland Clinic. She is accepting new patients and has indicated that she accepts telehealth appointments. Be sure to call ahead with Dr. Subramanian to book an appointment.
